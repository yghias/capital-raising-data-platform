# Use Cases

## Fund targeting
Rank investors and funds by fit against strategy, sector thesis, round size, geography, stage preference, and prior participation patterns. Output is a curated target list with rationale and confidence.

## Outreach prioritization
Combine relationship warmth, recency of signals, internal coverage, and investor fit to identify which prospects should be contacted now, by whom, and with what context.

## Fundraising performance
Measure funnel conversion, meeting velocity, diligence progression, investor response rates, and round close timing across teams, strategies, and time periods.

## Relationship intelligence
Map direct and indirect relationship paths across partners, founders, advisors, board members, current LPs, and historical co-investment networks.

## Investor segmentation
Cluster investors by strategy, check size, stage, geography, responsiveness, sector focus, and relationship profile to support more precise fundraising motions.

## Executive reporting
Provide board and leadership views of active fundraising status, target coverage, investor engagement, pipeline confidence, and forecasted time to milestone attainment.
