# Dashboard Metrics

## Executive dashboard metrics
- active fundraising round by company and target close date
- qualified investor targets by round and sector thesis
- investor coverage ratio and uncovered high-fit targets
- meetings booked, diligence progression, and pass rates
- forecasted milestone attainment based on current target quality and momentum

## Relationship intelligence metrics
- warm path availability for top-priority investors
- relationship recency and touchpoint depth
- board and advisor network leverage
- co-investment adjacency density

## Data product guidance
Expose metrics through curated semantic views rather than direct table access so business definitions remain stable as the physical model evolves.
